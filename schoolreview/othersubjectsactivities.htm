Bible Bowl

 

William and Beth have been studying the last four chapters of Deuteronomy and all of Joshua for this year’s Bible Bowl. It is proving to be very challenging material.

 

Field Trips:

February 10, 2007 – “Area” Baltimore First -  1st Place

March 17, 2007 – “Conference” Baltimore White Marsh – 1st Place

March 31, 2007 – “Union” Pittsburg, PA – 1st Place

April 21, 2007 – “NAD” Berrien Springs, MI – 2nd Place

 

Next year the competition will be on the book of Matthew and the accompanying Bible Commentary. Everyone is looking forward to this new material. William and Beth will be helping to develop a website (www.matthewpedia.com) which will contain study tools and ideas for next year’s event.
