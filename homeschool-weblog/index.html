Saturday, December 08, 2007

US Botanic Garden

Today we stumbled upon the Botanic Garden's Christmas exhibit. There was a large model train set up outside, and inside were models of famous DC buildings made entirely out of natural items like bark, acorns, corn husks, and pine cones. It was lots of fun. :)

Thursday, November 29, 2007

National Zoo

We spent a wonderful day at the zoo. One of the things that made it special was that we got to see it with a new friend of ours who visits the zoo often and knows the animals by name. Many of them recognized her and put on playful shows for us. It was a very special experience.
